    Dolphins

    They're curious, playful and incredibly intelligent. Learn about these beautiful mammals that call Sea World home.

    Did you know?

    • Dolphins are mammals and feed on fish.
    • At SeaWorld they can live to be beyond 50 years of age.
    • They can dive deeper than 450m and hold their breaths for over 5 minutes.
    • SeaWorld have one of the wolds larges lagoona-systems, with a natural sand-bottom.
    • Dolphins prefer warm, tropical temperatures.
    • At SeaWorld you can observe dolphins up close in a deep water encounter, and have your photograph taken with the animals.
    • Around 24 prosent of dolphins are threatened or near threatened.
    • SeaWorld is on duty 24/7 to rescue animals.
    • Most of the animals who are rescued are sendt back into the wild, but some animals can not survive on their own and gets a new home at SeaWorld.
